About Us

The Optical Sensing and Camera System (Oscars) Lab is based in the Faculty of Engineering at The University of Tokyo. The UTokyo-OscarsLab is a research lab for computer graphics/vision, led by Professor Yinqiang Zheng. Our lab is immersed in a unique environment in computer science, including computer graphics, computer vision, human-computer-interaction, software engineering, architecture, and hardware. We have access to rich resources in the collections of the university's libraries and facilities.
